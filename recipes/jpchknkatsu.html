Japanese Chicken Katsu

photo by Mitsuha Kirigaija

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Tonkatsu sauce for serving
  • Shredded cabbage for garnish
  • Optional: lemon wedges for serving
  • Optional: pickled ginger for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken by pounding it to an even thickness.
  2. Set up a breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Dredge the chicken in flour, dip in egg, and coat with panko.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Serve with tonkatsu sauce and shredded cabbage.
